About openfeed.icu

openfeed.icu is a small index of surveillance-style camera feeds that are reachable without a password or special client. we collect links and thumbnails, not footage. we do not host the streams, we do not run the hardware, and we do not help anyone break into anything.

the feeds here are framed like a public “live map” because that is how the open internet often presents them. that does not mean every placement on the map is exact, and it does not mean you should treat any of this as permission to harass people or show up somewhere. it is documentation of visibility, not an invitation.

we have been doing this in one form or another since 2019. the idea is blunt: if a camera is watching a public or semi-public space and the owner left the door open, someone should be able to see that it is happening. what you do with that knowledge is on you.

we do not accept donations. we do not run ads. we do not sell data. if that ever changes, this page will say so in boring plain language.

questions, takedown requests, or corrections: use the contact below. we are not fast, but we read mail. threats and legal theatre go in the same folder as spam.
